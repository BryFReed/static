Lookback Option

You lock in your strike up front, then automatically capture the absolute peak (or trough) the asset hits. It’s designed for anyone who wants to seize the best market swings without obsessing over timing.

Rainbow (Multi-Asset) Option

Bundle multiple assets in one trade and ride whichever one outperforms (or hedge the weakest). It’s a streamlined way to diversify or home in on the winner without juggling separate positions.

Range Option

Think of it as “buy low, sell high” on autopilot: you stand to gain from every up-and-down move over the term, no directional call required.
