CEO

Lihong Wang co-founded Freeport and defines its strategic direction, drawing on roles at Jane Street, IMC Financial Markets, and Akuna Capital. At Jane Street, he developed systematic options pricing signals; at IMC, he managed a discretionary single-stock options book; and at Akuna, he crafted hedging algorithms for exotic payoffs.
